Jainil being rich and foodie with no complicity,
Saral known for its name simplicity.

Kathan from Australia living in Sydney,
Kush lacking Knowledge which is obvious to me.

Manit, the cricketer in our class,

Mahek as her name ,fragrance,
Krishika laughing in a way to break dance.

Jiya the one they call topper,
And Kavya the they say yapper.

Sanvi probably the youngest in class,
Nidhi the one as silent as glass,

Nishika commands the room with a grace serene,
The undisputed title if the fashion queen.

To all the students of class 8C,
From this moment on you are now free.
